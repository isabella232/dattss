DaTtSs: Realtime Statistics Aggregation as a Service

You can't Fix what you can't Track

DaTtSs aggregates, stores and display in real time statistics captured from your systems. It helps you track counters, gauges or timers very easily and enables you to better understand your systems behaviours and status. For the ones who know it, DaTtSs is kind of a "statsd as a service".

Getting Started


Integrate DaTtSs in your apps by requiring the DaTtSs client library (NodeJS for now) and providing it with a process name as well as you AUTH_KEY.

Start aggregating counters, gauges and timers with just a line of code. You can go ahead! DaTtSs has very low overhead on your systems, it's been designed to be extremely efficient and lightweight.


Explore your Systems


As soon as your systems start streaming data to DaTtSs, you can track it in realtime. A few hours later you can see its evolution graphically. A few days later, you can even compare your daily values with averaged historical (last month) data.

How it works? Since we're using HTTP (instead of UDP for statsd and other simialr solutions), the client library pre-aggretages the statistics it receives before sending it periodically every 5s to a DaTtSs server. Each process running a DaTtSs client library only make one HTTP request every 5s which is super lightweight and won't overcharge your systems. DaTtSs is therefore able to aggregate an unlimited number of statistics at very high frequencies.
