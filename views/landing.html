DaTtSs: Realtime Statistics as a Service

You can't Fix what you can't Track

DaTtSs aggregates, stores and display in real time statistics captured from you apps. It helps you track counters, gauges or timers very easily to enable you to better understand your systems behaviour and status.
DaTtSs is currently in Private Beta. To get an Early Access, please do:
  • Get in touch at team@dattss.com
  • Join us at #dattss on irc.freenode.net

Getting Started


Integrate DaTtSs in your apps by requiring the DaTtSs client library and passing it the current process name as well as your private AUTH_KEY.

Start aggregating counters, gauges and timers with just a line. You can go ahead! DaTtSs has very low overhead on your website, it's been designed to be extremely efficient.


Explore your Systems


As soon as your systems start streaming data to DaTtSs, you can track it in realtime. A few hours later you can see its evolution graphically. A few days later, you can even compare your daily values with averaged historical (last month) data.
